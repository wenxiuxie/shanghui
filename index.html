我们的使命Our Mission

为华人企业搭建沟通、合作与发展的平台A Platform to Connect, Collaborate and Grow

我们为华盛顿州的华人企业提供交流共同利益的论坛，加强中美商界的沟通协作，并促进双向投资与跨境贸易。 We provide forums for Chinese businesses in Washington to discuss shared interests, strengthen US-China business communication and collaboration, and promote two-way investment and cross-border trade.

🤝

商业交流Business Networking

定期举办交流活动，帮助会员企业建立联系、识别共同利益、拓展商业人脉。Regular events that help members build relationships, identify common interests and expand their networks.

🌏

双向投资Two-Way Investment

促进中国对华盛顿州的投资，以及华盛顿州面向中国的投资，对接优质资源与项目。Promoting Chinese investment into Washington and Washington-bound investment into China, matching quality resources and projects.

📈

跨境贸易Cross-Border Trade

发展并便利中美之间的跨境贸易与商业往来，提供政策解读与实务支持。Developing and facilitating USA-China cross-border trade with policy insights and practical support.
