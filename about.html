关于华盛顿州华人总商会About CCCWA

一个连接华盛顿州与中国商界、服务本地华人企业的非营利平台。A nonprofit platform connecting the Washington and China business communities and serving local Chinese enterprises.

我们的使命Our Mission

促进中美投资、贸易与商业合作Advancing US-China Investment & Trade

华盛顿州华人总商会（CCCWA）是依据《华盛顿州非营利公司法》设立的非营利组织。我们致力于：The Chinese Chamber of Commerce in Washington State (CCCWA) is a nonprofit corporation established under the Washington Nonprofit Corporation Act. We are committed to:

  • 为华盛顿州的华人企业提供论坛，识别并探讨在美国的共同商业利益；Providing forums for Chinese businesses in Washington to identify and discuss common commercial interests in the United States;
  • 与州内其他组织就共同关心的事务开展合作，加强中美商界的双向沟通与协作；Working with other Washington organizations on matters of mutual interest, enhancing bilateral communication and collaboration between Chinese and American business communities;
  • 促进中国对华盛顿州的投资，以及华盛顿州面向中国的投资；Promoting Chinese investment into, and China-bound investment from, the State of Washington;
  • 发展并便利中美之间的跨境贸易与商业往来。Developing and facilitating USA-China cross-border trade and commerce.

我们做什么What We Do

商会的主要服务Our Key Services

①

论坛与交流Forums & Networking

定期举办研讨会、交流晚宴与联谊活动，帮助会员建立联系。Regular seminars, galas and networking events to help members connect.

②

投资与贸易对接Investment & Trade Matchmaking

组织考察团、对接项目资源，促进双向投资与跨境贸易。Delegations and project matchmaking to promote two-way investment and trade.
